The braid , or beger de brie, is a large breed of dog traditionlly used as herder and guardian of sheep.

Breed History

The briard, which is believed to have originated in France, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the French Amy as sentries, messengers and to search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sense sf hearing.
